- Difficulty capturing nuanced excellence within existing scoring system
- Limited feedback on review process
- Note-taking and final reporting becomes tedious, especially during exam periods

## 4. What They Value in Applications
- Genuine, unique personal motivation
- Continuity between applicant's background and proposed work
- Originality of proposed study
- Sincerity in personal statement
- Clear narrative of academic/research trajectory
- PhD proposals generally more compelling than Masters

## 5. Views on AI/Technology

- Wants AI to:
  - Conduct preliminary vetting of applications
  - Identify which applications need attention
  - Provide pre-populated rubric
- Maintains desire for human judgment and ability to override/modify
- Concerned about AI-generated motivational statements lacking authenticity

## 6. Suggestions & Ideas

- Allow more granular scoring between excellence levels
- Provide a pre-populated rubric from a trusted source
- Maintain human ability to adjust and provide narrative feedback
